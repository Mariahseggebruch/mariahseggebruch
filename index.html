Yard Hopper

Atlas School

Capstone project wrapping up my education at Atlas. My team and I simplified the process of finding AND promoting weekend sales. Tired of driving around looking for a good garage sale? Look no further. This single platform allows you to find sales in a list or a map view. When uploading your sale, you are able to add photots and select categories to attract those looking for specific items. Don't want to get on Facebook to use marketplace to find an estate sale? Yard Hopper is all you need. We used Figma for design, React Native for the interface as well as Expo for development. Our backend team used Express API, Firebase, Cloud Firestore.
